Extremely Chocolatey Gluten-Free Cupcakes


Recipe Makes 12


These decadent chocolate cupcakes are a true chocolate lover's dream.
They are moist, not overly sweet, and rich in cocoa powder and chocolate chips.
Additionally, they are super easy to make.
This recipe was actually the first ever recipe that I taught my daughter.



Ingredients For Cupcakes:

  • 300g ground almonds
  • 50g cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp (heaped) baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 80g dark soft brown sugar
  • 200ml milk (I use whole goat's milk)
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 200g dark chocolate chips

Ingredients For Chocolate Buttercream (Optional):

  • 250g (softened) unsalted butter
  • 450g icing sugar
  • 50g cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions For Cupcakes:

1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
2. Line the cupcake tray with cupcake cases.
3. Stir all ingredients together(until WELL combined).
4. Divide the batter evenly among the 12 cupcake cases.
5. Bake for around 15 minutes (until cupcakes are firm). This may vary slightly depending on your oven.
6. If you want to add buttercream, wait for the cupcakes to cool completely after taking them out of the oven.


Instructions For Chocolate Buttercream:

1. Beat the butter in a large bowl until soft.
2. Add vanilla extract, cocoa powder and milk and beat until smooth.
3. Gradually sift in the icing sugar until you reach your desired consistency.
